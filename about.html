Our Story.

Speed Gifts is Abu Dhabi's premier destination for personalized gifting. We believe that every gift should tell a story, and we are dedicated to helping you craft memories that last a lifetime.

Crafting Memories.

Located in the heart of Abu Dhabi at the WTC Mall, Speed Gifts was born out of a passion for bringing personal touches to everyday items. Over the years, we have mastered the art of custom engraving, personalized photo gifts, and unique creations designed for every special occasion.

We combine modern technology with traditional craftsmanship to ensure that whether you are purchasing a corporate gift in bulk or a single customized mug for a loved one, the quality is unparalleled and the message is crystal clear.
