Help & FAQ

Find answers to the most common questions about Speed Gifts.

How do I place a custom order?
You can place a custom order by contacting us directly via WhatsApp at +971 56 101 0387, or by visiting our store at WTC Mall, Abu Dhabi. Share your design idea and our team will guide you through the process.
What types of products can be customized?
We offer customization on a wide range of products including mugs, keychains, frames, cushions, wood engravings, acrylic prints, corporate gifts, and much more. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
How long does a custom order take?
Most custom orders are ready within 24–48 hours. Urgent orders can be accommodated depending on availability. For bulk or corporate orders, timelines may vary. Please contact us in advance.
Do you deliver across the UAE?
Yes! We offer fast delivery across all Emirates including Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, and Umm Al Quwain. Delivery charges and timelines may vary by location.
How can I contact Speed Gifts?
You can reach us via WhatsApp at +971 56 101 0387, by email at speedgiftsuae@gmail.com, or visit us at WTC Mall, Abu Dhabi. We're available Mon–Sat, 9AM to 8PM.
